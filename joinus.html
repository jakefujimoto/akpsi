Dear Rushees,

Alpha Kappa Psi provides ample opportunity to enhance the professional skills necessary to compete and succeed in the real world. As Alpha Kappa Psi is inclusive to all majors, the wide diversity of people contributes to a gold mine of experience and knowledge within the fraternity. There is undoubtedly an important commonality of motivation that drives the members of Alpha Kappa Psi to grow and achieve.

The Psi Epsilon chapter was founded at Loyola Marymount University in the spring of 2012. We continue to develop and modify our chapter in order to accommodate the means to professionalism for our newly established organization. This offers the unique opportunity for members to be heard should the want for change be prompted.

Given the invaluable resources that Alpha Kappa Psi has to offer on both a local and global scale, we invite you to attend our rush events and contact us without hesitation so that you too may be a part of this prestigious organization.

-Jake Fujimoto
